# Tutorials

A tutorial is a **lesson** — you follow along, you get a working result, and you learn the product by doing. Every tutorial here is verified end-to-end. If a step doesn't work for you on a real device, please [report it](https://github.com/aoneahsan/smsapp-docs/issues).

## Available tutorials

These are populated across our content batches. The full set will land soon — the planning + scaffolding shipped on 2026-05-10.

1. **Get started in 5 minutes** — sign in to the web dashboard, send your first SMS, see it land on a recipient phone.
2. **Send your first scheduled SMS** — schedule a one-off SMS for later today, watch the job run.
3. **Set up a small batch send** — upload a 10-row CSV, fire a batch, monitor delivery.
4. **Install the Android app and link it** — install from the Play Store, link it to your account, set it as a sender device.
5. **Become a volunteer device** — opt in (or out) of the default-on volunteer device pool, understand what that means.

## When you're done

By the end of all five tutorials, you will have:

- A signed-in account
- A linked Android device
- A successful one-off send, a successful scheduled send, and a successful batch send under your belt
- A clear understanding of where your phone fits into the volunteer device pool

After that, jump into [How-to guides](/how-to) for specific recipes, or [Explanation](/explanation) if you want to understand the design.
